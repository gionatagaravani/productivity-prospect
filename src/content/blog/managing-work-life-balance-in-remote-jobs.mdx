## Frequently Asked Questions


### How do I prevent overworking when my home is my office?

Set strict working hours and create a shutdown ritual at the end of each workday. This could include turning off work notifications, closing your laptop, and physically leaving your workspace. Treat your home office like a regular office - when you're done for the day, you leave it behind.



### What's the best way to maintain social connections while working remotely?

Schedule regular virtual coffee chats with colleagues, join online professional communities, and participate in virtual team-building activities. Also, make an effort to maintain in-person social connections outside of work hours with friends and family.



### How can I stay productive without burning out?

Use time management techniques like the Pomodoro method, take regular breaks, and ensure you're setting realistic goals. Remember to prioritize self-care and maintain healthy boundaries between work and personal time. Regular exercise and proper sleep are also crucial for sustained productivity.



### What should I do when work starts affecting my personal relationships?

Communicate openly with your loved ones about your work schedule and boundaries. Create designated quality time for family and friends, and be fully present during these moments by turning off work notifications. If necessary, consider adjusting your work schedule to better align with your personal life commitments.
