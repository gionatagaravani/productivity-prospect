## Frequently Asked Questions


### Q: How do I know if my work-life balance is affecting my mental health?

A: Common signs include persistent stress, difficulty sleeping, irritability, decreased productivity, and feeling overwhelmed. If you experience these symptoms consistently, it's important to assess your work-life balance and consider seeking professional help.



### Q: What are the most effective resources for immediate stress relief during work hours?

A: Quick-access resources include meditation apps, breathing exercise tools, short walks, and stress-relief exercises you can do at your desk. Many of these are available through free apps or websites.



### Q: How can I access mental health resources if my employer doesn't provide them?

A: There are numerous affordable or free resources available, including online therapy platforms, community mental health centers, support groups, and mental health hotlines. You can also explore local non-profit organizations that offer mental health services on a sliding scale.



### Q: What's the best way to maintain work-life balance while working remotely?

A: Key strategies include setting strict work hours, creating a dedicated workspace, taking regular breaks, maintaining social connections, and using time management tools. Visit our [remote work resources](https://productivityprospect.com/remote-work) for more detailed guidance.
