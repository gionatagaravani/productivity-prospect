## Frequently Asked Questions


### How long does it take to form productive habits?

Research suggests it takes anywhere from 21 to 66 days to form a new habit, with an average of about 66 days for a habit to become automatic. The key is consistency and starting small with manageable changes.



### What are the most effective ways to overcome procrastination?

The most effective strategies include breaking tasks into smaller, manageable chunks, using the 5-minute rule (starting a task for just 5 minutes), identifying and addressing the root cause of procrastination, and creating accountability systems.



### How can I stay productive when working from home?

Maintain a dedicated workspace, establish clear boundaries between work and personal life, follow a consistent schedule, take regular breaks, and use technology tools to stay connected and organized. Regular communication with team members also helps maintain accountability.



### What should I do when I feel overwhelmed with too many tasks?

Start by brain dumping all tasks onto paper, prioritize using the Eisenhower Matrix, break down large projects into smaller tasks, delegate where possible, and focus on completing one task at a time. Don't hesitate to communicate with stakeholders if deadlines need adjustment.
